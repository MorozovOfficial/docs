<Tip>
  How stories are created — from idea to publication.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Idea">
    A story starts with an idea: a theme, a plot, characters, or a general direction. Sometimes the idea comes from you, and sometimes from our team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    We look at the idea, its potential, and how well it fits the project’s style and content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preparation">
    We build the foundation of the story: characters, visual style, atmosphere, and overall direction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Development">
    We shape the plot, key scenes, chapter structure, and the format of the material.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Writing">
    We create the story itself — this can be an original story, fanfiction, a comic, or another format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Editing">
    We review the text, improve the wording, remove inaccuracies, and refine the details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publication">
    After final preparation, the story is published on the site along with any extra materials if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
